About Us

Bring a Smile aims to help and support holisitc development of a child(physical, mental, intellectual, emotional, social and spiritual). Moreover, to give the Filipino child the basic right to enjoy a happy, safe, andfulfilling future. It was founded in 2018. Everyone who works in this organization firmly believes and upholds the values of compassion and commitment, integrity and teamwork in working for the welfare of street children.
